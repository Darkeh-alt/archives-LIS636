Originally from North Carolina, I went to California in order to study for my Undergraduate Degree in International Relations at the University of Southern California. After completing my degree, I decided to come back home to study for my Master's Degree in Library and Information Science at the University of North Carolina Greensboro.
Through my studies, I have developed a passion for international history and research which has translated into wanting to assist with college students in their research needs. I have also received experience in accounting and bookkeeping which has given me passion into Business librarianship.
Currently I am finishing up my Master's Degree and will be doing an 11-month contract with the JAARS Museum in their Archives.
